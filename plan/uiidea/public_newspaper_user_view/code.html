NCC Bank Strengthens Digital Transformation with FloraBank CBS Upgrade

26 August, 2025 • ICT Division

NCC Bank has successfully completed a major upgrade of its Core Banking System (CBS) from version 18.4 to 22.4, ensuring system modernization, enhanced stability, and stronger regulatory compliance. As part of this initiative, compliance with Bangladesh Bank control points improved significantly from 72 percent to 93 percent, strengthening audit readiness and regulatory adherence.

The upgrade introduced several critical functional enhancements, including Group Limit Management, Multiple Loan Rescheduling, an enhanced Customer Security Framework, Auto Loan Settlement mechanisms, Multi-Currency support, Centralized Dashboard and Reporting, Locker Management services, an integrated SMS Notification system, Write-off Management, and a robust Anti-Money Laundering (AML) solution.

Offshore Banking Unit CBS Migration Strengthens International Operations

20 October, 2025 • EA-News, NCC Bank

NCC Bank has successfully migrated its Offshore Banking Unit (OBU) Core Banking System (CBS), ensuring full compliance with international banking standards. The migration enhances transaction processing, strengthens system security, and reinforces operational resilience.

With this upgrade, the bank's offshore banking capabilities are significantly improved, providing a reliable and efficient platform for international clients. This initiative reflects NCC Bank's commitment to delivering secure, modern, and globally compliant banking services.

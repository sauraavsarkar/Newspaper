# Project Brief: Chronicle OS – The Newsroom Operating System

## 1. Project Overview
**Chronicle OS** is a specialized newsroom operating system designed for business-focused newspapers. Unlike standard content management systems, Chronicle OS is built as an enterprise SaaS platform that prioritizes editorial efficiency, real-time data visualization, and monetization control. It bridges the gap between news creation (Journalists/Editors) and business operations (Marketing/Ad Managers).

---

## 2. Core Value Proposition
- **Role-Based Efficiency:** Dynamic dashboards tailored to specific newsroom roles (Admin, Editor-in-Chief, Journalist, SEO Analyst).
- **Monetization-First Design:** Direct integration of ad management and revenue tracking into the editorial workflow.
- **Data-Driven Publishing:** Real-time analytics and engagement heatmaps to inform content strategy.
- **Structured Workflow:** A rigorous pipeline from ideation to publication via a metadata-rich Kanban system.

---

## 3. Key Modules & Features

### A. Editorial Management
- **Journalist Workspace:** Personalized KPIs (read time, engagement), assignment tracking with countdown deadlines, and a dedicated feedback loop.
- **Editorial Kanban:** An 8-stage pipeline (Idea → Draft → Assigned → Editing → Review → Approved → Scheduled → Published) for granular control over the content lifecycle.
- **Notion-style Editor:** A block-based writing environment featuring an "Intelligence Hub" with live SEO scoring, readability analysis, and AI headline optimization.

### B. Business Intelligence & Ad Operations
- **Revenue Dashboard:** Real-time tracking of ad revenue, RPM, and CTR across sponsored and affiliate content.
- **Visual Ad Manager:** A grid-based layout for assigning and scheduling ad slots directly on the page structure.
- **Yield Optimization:** Detailed logs for tracking slot performance and A/B testing placement strategies.

### C. Audience Analytics
- **Geographic Distribution:** Global reader heatmaps to identify high-traffic regions.
- **Engagement Heat-Grid:** Peak reading hours by category (Tech, Politics, Lifestyle).
- **Predictive Trends:** Tracking "Rising Keywords" and "Viral Potential" to stay ahead of the news cycle.

---

## 4. Visual Identity & UX Principles
- **Design System:** *Editorial Command* – A high-density, card-based interface.
- **Typography:** Bold serif headlines for authority; high-readability sans-serif (Geist) for data and body copy.
- **Aesthetic:** Professional, enterprise-grade, and "data-heavy yet clean," drawing inspiration from Trello, Notion, and Google Analytics.
- **Device Support:** Primary optimization for Desktop (Newsroom Ops) with responsive Public Views for readers.

---

## 5. Technical Requirements (Conceptual)
- **Real-time Updates:** Livewire or WebSockets for live traffic and revenue tickers.
- **Collaboration:** Inline commenting system and change requests for multi-user editing.
- **Automation:** AI-driven tagging and auto-scheduling based on traffic predictions.

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Throughput:** Increased speed from draft to publication.
- **Revenue:** Improved RPM through optimized ad placements.
- **Engagement:** Higher average read times driven by data-informed story selection.
